What is Digital Citizenship?

Digital Citizenship refers to responsible and ethical use of technology. It includes online safety, respectful communication, and protecting personal information.

  • Understanding digital rights and responsibilities
  • Practicing safe and responsible online behavior
  • Recognizing cyberbullying and how to prevent it
  • Protecting personal information online
